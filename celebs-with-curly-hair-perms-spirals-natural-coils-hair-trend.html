Celebs With Curly Hair: Perms, Spirals, Natural Coils Hair Trend

Publish date: 2024-08-02

Celebs are all about that curl! From runways to red carpets, long gone are the days of pin straight tresses and super sleek strands. Instead, bouncy ringlets, curly coifs and undone waves that are both natural and curling-iron made are where it’s at.

We can always count on stars like Emmy Rossum, Lupita Nyong’o and Yara Shahidi to play up their natural coils. Emmy even documents the ups and downs of caring for her ‘do on social media — asking fans for product recommendations and sharing her own finds. Filing it under things we never thought we’d say, Hollywood is in the midst of a perm revolution with some Tinseltown’s leading ladies (think: Olivia Munn, Jaime King and Emma Stone) reviving the ‘80s hair treatment in a totally modern (and chic!) way. And, obviously, there are countless other stars going for curling-iron-made texture and rocking cute spiral styles both on and off the red carpet.
