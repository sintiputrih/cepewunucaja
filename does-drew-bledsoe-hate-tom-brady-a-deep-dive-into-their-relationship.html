Does Drew Bledsoe Hate Tom Brady?: A Deep Dive Into Their Relationship

Publish date: 2024-02-11

Drew Bledsoe and Tom Brady, two quarterbacks who should both be envious of each other, have instead cultivated a companionship contrary to the legend. Their relationship extends beyond Brady taking Bledsoe's job as the starter at quarterback when Bledsoe went down with what turned out to be a season-ending injury. In just a few minutes we can both figure out what issues Tom Brady and Bledsoe have with one another, as well as some shocking revelations of their relationship as was attested to by the guests on the recent Tom Brady roast on Netflix.


The Quarterbacks at a Glance

First overall draft pick by the New England Patriots in 1993, Drew Bledsoe took the Patriots to a Super Bowl and was named to three Pro Bowls in his first five years in the league.
In 2001, Bledsoe was injured, lost his job, and was replaced by sixth-round pick Tom Brady, who went on to become a legend in the NFL.


The Ski Trip Incident

It happened late in a ski trip last when Bledsoe plowed through a ski turn needed to avoid seemingly the only 3-year-old on the entire map and body-sprayed Gisele Bündchen, Tom Brady's ex-wife, so badly with snow that a weird moment became a rare nice one. Nonetheless, Bündchen hugged Bledsoe, saying how much he is revered in their home, an endorsement and reflection of the respect that binds Bledsoe and Brady.


The Tom Brady Roast

Bledsoe recently also showcased his camaraderie and mutual respect with Brady through wittier sensibilities at Brady's roast on Netflix. Despite how Brady took over being the Patriots' starting quarterback, Bledsoe remained calm and comical, reflecting their respect and friendship. The pair hugged it out, and embraced, showing how much they respect one another.


The Mutual Respect

For both quarterbacks, their interactions with one another have been supportive, and while they were professional competitors, there is a strong, respectful relationship at its core, which ultimately explains why Bledsoe and Brady are still on good terms to this day.
Ultimately, the dynamic, which developed between Drew Bledsoe and Tom Brady is one of trust, respect, and camaraderie. The reverence afforded to their interactions over the years and the insights revealed at the recent Tom Brady roast merely underlines the strength of their friendship - and shoot down any theory that they might be out for each other.
