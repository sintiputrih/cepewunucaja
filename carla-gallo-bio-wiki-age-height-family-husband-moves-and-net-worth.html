Carla Gallo Biography

Carla Gallo (born Carla Paolina Gallo) is an American actress. Gallo has had recurring roles in the television series Undeclared, Carnivàle, Bones, Californication, and a number of film roles. Since her role on Undeclared, she has made frequent appearances in other Judd Apatow productions.

Carla Gallo Age
