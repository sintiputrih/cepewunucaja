Pippa Middleton Attends Wimbledon 2019 with Meghan Markle &amp; Kate Middleton

Publish date: 2024-03-31
  • Pippa Middleton stepped out alongside Kate Middleton and Meghan Markle for the women’s singles final at Wimbledon on July 13, 2019.
  • Pippa accompanied her sister, the Duchess of Cambridge, and sat next to the Duchess of Sussex.
  • Pippa attended Wimbledon with her husband, James Matthews, earlier this week. She wore the prettiest yellow wrap dress by GANNI on that occasion.

The Wimbledon women’s singles final was already set to be a star-studded event, with both Catherine, Duchess of Cambridge, and Meghan, Duchess of Sussex, in attendance. But also making an appearance alongside the duchesses was Kate’s sister, Pippa Middleton.

Related Story

Having made a seriously chic visit to Wimbledon earlier this week, Pippa stepped out wearing an incredibly pretty white dress, covered in a blue floral print. She wore her hair in a side plait and looked elated to be sitting in the Royal Box with both Meghan and Kate.

According to @KateMCambridge, Pippa’s dress is by Anna Mason and is made to order. She completed the look with Garrard Petal Ear Climbers, Ray-Ban shades, and a Cartier watch.

Adam Davy - PA Images//Getty Images

Earlier in the week, Pippa wore a gorgeous—and affordable—yellow dress by GANNI, covered in a summery sunflower print. She completed the chic look with a white beaded bag, block-heel sandals (also white), and tortoiseshell shades.

You can even still shop Pippa’s GANNI wrap dress for just $205, but it’s sure to sell out now that she’s worn it.

Neil Mockford//Getty Images

GANNI Floral-Print Wrap Midi Dress

Prior to attending the sporting event with her husband, Pippa was seen alongside her brother, James Middleton. She looked incredibly stylish in a pink Stella McCartney dress with a coordinating hat designed by Luisa Kelsey. She completed the look with a J.Crew rattan straw clutch.
