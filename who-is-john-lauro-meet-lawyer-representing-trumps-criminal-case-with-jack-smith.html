Who is John Lauro? Meet lawyer representing Trumps criminal case with Jack Smith

Publish date: 2024-08-07

On Thursday, John Lauro and other attorneys for former President Donald Trump met with prosecutors at special counsel Jack Smith’s office to discuss the possibility of a third charge.

On Thursday, John Lauro and Todd Blanche, attorneys for Trump, met with Smith’s group. CNN reported that a Smith prosecutor and jurors could be seen entering the courtroom. The former POTUS’ team left the conference without receiving any information regarding the timing of a potential third indictment.

According to Axios, these meetings give defense attorneys a chance to present their final defense against any prospective charges that their client may be subject to.

Trump fought against having a meeting between his lawyers and Smith’s team after receiving a target letter from Smith earlier this month. The 45th president thought that the meeting was pointless because the indictment had already been announced.

Who Is John Lauro 

John Lauro was the founding member and designated partner of the legal organization Lauro & Singer is Lauro. He was raised in New York City and eventually received a Georgetown University magna cum laude.

He was chosen to serve as an Advisory Neighborhood Commissioner in the District of Columbia while still in college. He attended the Georgetown University Law Center and received a magna cum laude degree. The Georgetown Law Journal had him on its editorial board.

He resumed his legal career with the global law firm Morgan, Lewis, and Bockius after moving back to New York. Later, he opened a practice in Florida and joined forces with a big law firm there as a partner. He established The Lauro Law Firm in 1994; it is currently known as Lauro & Singer. Offices for the legal firm are located in Florida and New York.

Lauro is described as “an accomplished trial lawyer with experience in complex criminal and commercial litigation” on the website of the law firm. In addition to other white-collar and commercial litigation challenges, Mr. Lauro handles instances concerning conspiracy, healthcare, and accounting.

John Lauro has handled some pretty high-profile cases in the past, including helping the former president of a national healthcare company obtain a Rule 29 acquittal in a high-profile federal criminal case and defending a defendant in the first “dot-com securities fraud” case, which ended in a jury acquittal on all counts.
