Where to find all the Gears in Twilight Harbor | Spyro Reignited Trilogy

Publish date: 2024-05-04
Recommended Videos

Another day, another achievement to get in Spyro Reignited Trilogy.

In Twilight Harbor Spyro 1, the game will ask you to search for six small hidden Gears around the map and burn them to get an achievement. These Gears are hidden in pretty good locations, so it helps to know where to look.

All The Gears Location In Twilight Harbor

Here is where to find the six Gears in the level. Just a word of warning though, if you die while collecting these Gears in any part of the level, you will be forced to retrace your steps and get all the Gears once again, so it might be the best clearing the level of any hazards first before attempting to collect all six.

While most of the Gears are easy to find, one will require you to lift up a bridge near the boost hill at the end of the level to glide towards it as it is pretty high up.

Most of the Gears, however, are pretty easy to get and once you burn them all with your fire breath, the achievement should unlock a few moments later.
