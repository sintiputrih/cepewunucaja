Top 10 Soccer Players with the Biggest Instagram Followings in 2024

Publish date: 2024-02-20

In the world of soccer, the influence of players extends beyond the pitch and onto social media platforms, particularly Instagram. With the sport's global appeal, it comes as no surprise that soccer players are among the most followed individuals on Instagram. In 2024, the top 10 soccer players with the biggest Instagram followings are as follows:

1. Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo, the legendary Portuguese forward, continues to dominate Instagram with an astounding 616 million followers. Renowned for his exceptional goal-scoring abilities and numerous accolades, including 5 FIFA Ballon d'Or awards, 5 UEFA Champions League titles, and multiple domestic league triumphs, Ronaldo's Instagram following has grown significantly, solidifying his position as the most followed soccer player on the platform.

2. Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi, often hailed as one of the greatest footballers of all time, boasts a substantial Instagram following of 500 million. As a key figure in the soccer world, Messi's achievements, including 2022 World Cup success and eight Ballon d’Or titles, have undoubtedly contributed to his massive social media presence.

3. Mohamed Salah

Liverpool's Egyptian winger, Mohamed Salah, has secured the third position with over 63.3 million Instagram followers. Known for his exceptional speed and goal-scoring prowess, Salah's contributions to Liverpool's successes in the UEFA Champions League and the Premier League have garnered him a significant online following.

4. Neymar

Neymar, the Brazilian forward renowned for his flair and technical ability, maintains a strong presence on Instagram with a follower count of 219 million. His flashy style of play and success at both club and international levels have undoubtedly contributed to his substantial social media following.

5. Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli, the Indian cricket star, has crossed over into the soccer realm, securing the fifth position with a remarkable 266 million followers. Kohli's influence extends beyond cricket, and his association with soccer has garnered him a significant following on Instagram.

6. Cristiano Pulisic

Cristiano Pulisic, the American soccer prodigy, has amassed an impressive Instagram following. As a rising star in the sport with significant contributions to both club and country, Pulisic's social media presence reflects his growing influence in the soccer world.

7. Kylian Mbappe

Kylian Mbappe, the French forward known for his electrifying pace and clinical finishing, has achieved a notable Instagram following. His performances on the field, particularly during the 2022 World Cup, have contributed to his increasing popularity on social media.

8. Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi, the Argentine maestro, has secured the eighth position with a remarkable 500 million followers. As a key figure in the soccer world, Messi's achievements, including 2022 World Cup success and eight Ballon d’Or titles, have undoubtedly contributed to his massive social media presence.

9. LeBron James

LeBron James, the iconic basketball player, has ventured into the soccer social media landscape with a significant Instagram following of 159 million. His global appeal transcends basketball, and his engagement with soccer has earned him a notable presence on the platform.

10. Sergio Ramos

Sergio Ramos, the Spanish defender, rounds off the top 10 with over 62.5 million Instagram followers. With a decorated career that includes multiple Champions League and domestic league titles, Ramos' influence extends beyond the pitch, as evident from his substantial social media following.

The top 10 soccer players with the biggest Instagram followings in 2024 represent a diverse array of talent, each contributing to the global appeal of the sport and maintaining a significant presence on the world's most popular photo-sharing platform. Their influence extends far beyond the confines of the soccer field, showcasing the power of social media in amplifying their reach and impact on a global scale.
