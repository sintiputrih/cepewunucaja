Is Gearbox making Borderlands 4?

Publish date: 2024-04-30

The announcement of a potential Borderlands 4 has sparked excitement and speculation among fans of the beloved franchise. Courtesy of Fan Arch, here's an analysis of the current status of Borderlands 4 based on the latest information available.

Confirmation from Gearbox Enterprises President

Gearbox Enterprises President, Randy Pitchford, has confirmed that Borderlands 4 is indeed in development at the studio. In one of his tweets, he mentioned that they are working on "the big one," implying the next installment of the Borderlands series.

Development Status and Release Window

It's important to note that the exact commencement of the development of Borderlands 4 remains undisclosed. Also, Gearbox has not provided any specific release window for the game, implying that it could still be a while before Borderlands 4 is released.

Time Gap and Speculation

Considering the extended wait between Borderlands 2 and 3, coupled with behind-the-scenes financial drama at Embracer, it's reasonable to assume that the release of Borderlands 4 might still be some time away. 

But there's speculation that Borderlands 4 could shy away from major post-launch expansions and adopt prolonged seasonal content and Battle Passes.

Storyline and Characters

Given the hints from Borderlands 3's DLC, it is suggested that the storyline of Borderlands 4 will pick up where Borderlands 3 left off, possibly fast-forwarding a few years, similar to the time gap between the release of the two games. There are also speculations about the involvement of iconic characters like Lilith and the role of new characters such as Ava in the upcoming game.

Cross-Platform and Game Features

According to sources, Borderlands 4 will be cross-platform, as Gearbox Software has built its own cross-play infrastructure. The franchise is renowned for its emphasis on randomly generated firearms, and it is expected that this feature will continue in Borderlands 4.

The game might also adopt new features in line with the evolving gaming landscape, potentially including elements of the "live service" model.

Gearbox's Focus on Borderlands 4

It has been reported that development of Borderlands 4 became Gearbox's priority after a "creative shakeup" that resulted in several other in-development projects being shelved in favor of the iconic franchise. This indicates the studio's strong commitment to the success of Borderlands 4.

Film Adaptation

Interestingly, fans of the franchise can look forward to a film adaptation of Borderlands, scheduled to arrive in theaters in August 2024.

The film boasts an impressive cast, including Cate Blanchett, Kevin Hart, Jack Black, and Jamie Lee Curtis, and is directed by Eli Roth. The release of the film adds another dimension of excitement for fans eagerly awaiting the next installment of the game series.

 

The confirmation of Borderlands 4 in development by Gearbox Enterprises President, Randy Pitchford, has generated significant anticipation among fans.

While specific details about the game's development status and release window remain undisclosed, the various hints and speculations surrounding the storyline, characters, game features, and the studio's focus on the project indicate a strong commitment to delivering an exceptional gaming experience with Borderlands 4.

For fans of the franchise, the upcoming film adaptation further adds to the excitement surrounding the Borderlands universe.
