Huma Amir Shah - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-06-14

Pakistani television host, anchor, and producer who is best recognized for her work with the Geo TV Network as their head news programming anchor. She is also known for her work with the BOL network where she was not only an anchor but a Senior Content Manager as well. 

Before Fame

She earned her Master's degree in Strategic Studies from the Quaid-e-Azam University in Islamabad. She started her career in television in 2005 when she worked as an anchor and producer for AAJ TV. Then in 2009 she joined the Express 24/7 network. 

Trivia

She is also known for her twitter presence and shares news driven articles as well as photos from her television appearances for her over 70,000 followers. 

Family Life

She was born and raised in Islamabad, Karachi. Her husband's name is Tez Aftab.

Associated With

Other anchors and presenters also known for their work with the Geo TV network include Rabia Anum and Maria Memon.
