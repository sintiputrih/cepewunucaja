MJF Eviscerates Indiana Before AEW Dynamite This Week

Publish date: 2024-03-11

MJF is one of the best talkers in the industry and there’s no denying that. The AEW World Champion never shies away from berating fans and opponents. He’s turned his attention towards Indiana and its citizens.

MJF tweeted out a huge diss towards Indiana, which is set to host AEW Dynamite this Wednesday. The Devil Himself eviscerated the midwestern state in his latest tweet.

Everyone in Indiana is married to their sister and they all masturbate with corn cobs. I’m not worried about them reading this tweet because people from Indiana don’t know how to read.

MJF is set for AEW Dynamite at Indiana Farmers Coliseum this Wednesday. It remains to be seen what MJF will have in store for the fans in attendance and watching at home.
