Here's How Sebastian Bear-McClard Got So Rich

Publish date: 2024-01-15

Highlights

  • Emily Ratajkowski married a non-celebrity, raising eyebrows about her intentions, but she has always chosen her partners on her own terms.
  • Sebastien Bear-McClard faced rent scandal allegations, with a reported $15 million net worth that fueled speculation about Ratajkowski's motives.
  • Bear-McClard is an independent movie producer with a family background in art, and Ratajkowski defended him during his controversies.

When Emily Ratajkowski married her now ex-husband, Sebastian Bear-McClard, little was known about him. The former couple surprised a lot of fans when they had a city hall wedding in 2018, given that Ratajkowski and Bear-McClard had not been dating for that long. More surprising still to fans was that Bear-McClard appeared to be very out of Ratajkowski's league.

How Ratajkowski ended up with someone who's relatively unknown is a bit of a mystery. But the Inamorata swimsuit line owner has been one to always date non-celebrities. At times, Ratajkowski's intentions have been questioned. But Ratajkowksi has never been one to defend her choices about who she dates, living life by her own terms.

In 2019, Bear-McClard made headlines for allegedly owing his landlord $120,000 for dodging about two years of rent in a $4,900-a-month apartment in NoHo, an expensive location in Manhattan, New York. The scandal had fans wondering who this man was. It was not until Ratajkowski made a statement, that fans learned more about Bear-McClard.

Here's what we know.

How Much Sebastian Bear-McClard Is Worth

Net Worth: $15 Million

The reason many fans used to believe that Ratajkowski was in it for Bear-McClard's money was because of his once reported $15 million net worth. That is nearly double the model's net worth, which is estimated to be around $8 million. But the two had known each other for quite some time before they got together. They'd been in the same group of friends for years. Ratajkowski even said that she already had a feeling Bear-McClard might be "the one" before they started dating.

"We knew each other for a long time before and he [Bear-McClard] likes to joke like, 'Yeah, everyone thinks we got married quickly but you vetted me for two years," she told Busy Tonight. "Women always know ... I was always like 'Okay, I probably shouldn't hang out with that guy alone' ... and then next thing you know I'm at the courthouse getting married."

Sebastian Bear-McClard Comes From A Family Of Artists

Sebastian Bear-McClard's mother is a filmmaker, writer and activist and his father is a modern-art artist

When Ratajkowski came to Bear-McClard's defense during the rent scandal, she wrote on Twitter, "He was raised in the neighborhood he lives in now, both of his parents are artists who were priced out of their homes in downtown New York."

Indeed, Bear-McClard's mother Liza Bear is a filmmaker, writer, and activist. She co-founded the '70s art magazines Avalance and Bomb. Michael McClard, his father, is an artist who's had exhibits at New York City's Museum of Modern art.

Sources once said that Bear alone had a total net worth of $96 million as of 2021, mostly from his directing career. There aren't that many public details about McClard's works and net worth. However, Em Rata denied rumors of her husband's privileged upbringing. She even pulled the struggling artist card in defending him during the rent controversy.

Sebastian Bear-McClard Is An Independent Movie Producer

Sebastian Bear-McClard has produced 11 projects

"He’s an independent movie producer so people thinking he’s rich is real nice but not based in fact," Ratajkowski wrote on Twitter in defense of her husband.

Bear-McClard has in fact worked as a director, writer, producer, and actor. He is a partner of the production company Elara Pictures with the Safdie Brothers and Oscar Boyson. He also produced the American crime thriller Uncut Gems starring Adam Sandler.

The movie earned an impressive $50 million. Sales for the ticket were even described as "a testament to the power of an original and exhilarating movie with an unexpected performance from an iconic comedy star" by senior Comscore analyst, Paul Dergarabedian. But Bear-McClard's other projects weren't really as huge.

He produced the psychological drama film Heaven Knows What in 2014 and Good Time starring Robert Pattinson in 2017. These films earned around 1/4 or even less of what Uncut Gems made.
