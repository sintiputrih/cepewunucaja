Here's What Costco's Food Court Menu Looks Like in 8 Countries

Publish date: 2024-08-01

No one can resist the Costco food court. Juicy hot dogs for just $1.50? We’ll take two. Massive slices of pizza for $1.99 each? Yes, please. Grabbing a $1.49 churro (or two) to eat while you shop the warehouse retailer? Every time. And did we mention you can catch a free show at the Costco food court, too? That’s right, if you haven’t watched the sauce robot make a Costco pizza at least once, you’ve been missing out. It’s a magical place, the Costco food court. But little did we know, the Costco food court in the United States might just pale in comparison to those Costco food court menus found in other parts of the world.

Recently on the Costco subreddit, users have been posting the full food court menu from their local Costco. We’re talking menus from Australia, Japan, Mexico, and more — and we need some of these food court offerings stat.

Take, for instance, the food court menu in Perth, Australia: Here, the Costco food courts serve everything from poke-style salad bowls to mango smoothies. And over in Japan? You can order Aussie beef pies, pulled pork burgers and caramel soft serve.

Ahead, we’ve gathered Costco food court menus from around the world.

Australia

Everything on this menu is a must-order.

“Different pizzas, Bahn mi, Poke bowls, donut balls, chicken wings & mango smoothie… Bring it to the US!”

Canada

One word: poutine.

“Oh I miss that poutine,” writes one commenter. “Don’t understand why poutine haven’t become a common thing in the US. Gravy, cheese curds, and fries. Not an exotic concept.

England

In Birmingham, Costco food court menus are stacked, boasting everything from gelato and sorbet ice cream to jacket potatoes and cottage pie.

Iceland

We see you, cheeseburgers and gelato.

“One bite and I was hooked!” writes one Reddit user of the cheeseburgers. “I wasn’t a huge fan of the mango smoothie.”

Japan

“That Aussie pie sounds bomb,” comments one Reddit user. Agreed.

Korea

The Costco food courts in Korea sell everything from fried rice and sweet pumpkin soup to bulgogi bakes and greek yogurt granola.

“Persimmon smoothie and bulgogi bake??? Do I need to go to Korea just for Costco lunch???” comments one Reddit user.

Mexico

Our favorite item on the Mexico Costco food court menu? The mango smoothies.

Spain

View post on imgur.com

The food court in Madrid has it all: vanilla sundaes, cobb salads, croquetas, barbacoa chicken wings, pizza, chicken fingers and fries, and chicken bakes. And we love to see it.
