    Bo Welch Production Designer

    Welch began his career by working as a production designer on the Tim Burton films Edward Scissorhands, Beetlejuice, and Batman Returns, also as on the Barry Sonnenfeld films Men in Black and Wild Wild West, He made his directorial debut with The Cat in the Hat but it was a critical and commercial dissatisfaction.

    Welch has been appointed for four Academy Awards for Best Art Direction-Set Decoration, three of them shared with set decorator Cheryl Carasik and another one with J. Michael Riva and Linda DeScenna. The four films are namely, Men in Black, The Birdcage, A Little Princess with Carasik, and The Color Purple with Riva and DeScenna.
