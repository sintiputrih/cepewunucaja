Rihanna Celebrates 28th Birthday-Rihanna's Birthday Party

Publish date: 2024-06-20

Despite canceling her Grammys performance and postponing her Anti tour, Rihanna still had something to celebrate on Saturday: her 28th birthday. 

The superstar fêted her big day at Via Alloro in Beverly Hills, Us Weekly reports. Her best friend Melissa Forde was one of the many attendees, and shared some videos from the party. The best may be a candid moment of the birthday girl singing and twerking to Fetty Wap's "My Way," in a silk dress, wine glass in hand.

After a weekend of celebration, Rihanna posted a grateful tweet yesterday:
