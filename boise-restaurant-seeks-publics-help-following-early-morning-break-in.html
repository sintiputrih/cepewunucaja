Boise restaurant seeks public's help following early-morning break-in

Publish date: 2024-09-05

BOISE, Idaho (CBS2) — A Boise restaurant is asking for your help.

Someone broke into Alyonka Russian Cuisine around 2:50 a.m. on Monday. The restaurant posted photos of the damage and surveillance images on Facebook.

"We're devastated, but we believe in the strength of our community," the Facebook post said.

Anyone with information on the break-in is asked to call Boise Police.
