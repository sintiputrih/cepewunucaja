Keke Palmer Subtly Reveals the Sex of Her Baby

Publish date: 2024-09-27

Keke Palmer is having a boy!

The actress appeared as a guest on the latest episode of The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon, and during her interview, she casually dropped the news of her baby's sex—and zodiac sign.

While talking about astrological signs, Palmer said, "My baby is either going to be a Pisces or an Aries." She added, "Pisces are known to be very deep, they're emotional creatures. So I just want to make sure I'm not too blunt for my baby boy."

Palmer confirmed her pregnancy back in December, hilariously debuting her baby bump while hosting Saturday Night Live. The boy will be her first child with boyfriend Darius Jackson, whom she started dating around May 2021.

"Every time I look back at that clip, I'm like, 'Why do I sound like a villain?'" Palmer told Jimmy Fallon, remembering her bump reveal. "I don't know why I said it like this, but I was so excited."

Getty Images

Ahead of the birth of their baby, Palmer and Jackson took a dreamy, romantic babymoon to a tropical destination. The actress shared photos of the getaway and wrote an emotional caption about embracing the slow, simple joys in life.

"This season I am going to learn how to really take it easy. how to go slow (or slower than I have which is speedy for the average ♍️) because it definitely doesn't mean my drive and passion will end, just transform and evolve through a different tool," she wrote. "The point is. everything that's going to come is already in motion. all i have to do is enjoy. be grateful and reap the benefits of those that came before me, all the sacrifices, all the prayers and all the power to be intentional and at EASE."
