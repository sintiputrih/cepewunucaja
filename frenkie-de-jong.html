Frenkie de Jong Biography

Frenkie de Jong was born on May 12, 1997, in Arkel, Netherlands, the same town where Brazilian player Marcelo was born. Frankie’s mother and father were both professional players, therefore coming from a family with a strong professional football heritage, he got the proper instruction and supervision from an early age. He grew up playing football for his local club, ASV Arkel, and he was able to balance his studies and sports well. In 2004, he joined the Willem II, where he developed the elements that characterize his current style of play, such as his seamless ability to make the most difficult tasks appear hilariously simple.
