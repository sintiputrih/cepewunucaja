Biography

Kirk Hammett was born in San Francisco, CA on November 18, 1962. Lead guitarist for the heavy metal band Metallica, the group known for albums like Kill ‘Em All. He was also a member of Exodus, a thrash metal outfit. He is joined by singer/guitarist James Hetfield, bassist Robert Trujillo and drummer Lars Ulrich in Metallica. He worked at Burger King to make enough money to buy a Marshall amplifier. He formed his first band in 1980.
