Jeffrey Omen death :Know More About Him

Publish date: 2024-06-16

Jeffrey M. Omen, lovingly known as Jeff, was a beloved resident of Beaver Dam, Wisconsin. He was renowned for his extraordinary Halloween house, considered the best in Beaver Dam and even Dodge County. Unfortunately, Jeff passed away on October 9, 2023.

Who Was Jeffrey M. Omen?

Born on May 24, 1955, Jeff was a cherished member of his local community. He resided at 508 Prospect Ave, Beaver Dam, WI and his passion for Halloween and creative home decorations brought joy to his neighbors and visitors alike. His Facebook profile suggests that he was an active participant in local community events.

How Did jeffrey Die?

The cause of Jeff’s death has not been disclosed in the public domain. What is known is that he passed away on October 9, 2023. His obituary can be found on the websites of Koepsell Funeral Home and Tribute Archive.

Tributes pours to his death

A service was held in Jeff’s honor on October 17, 2023, at Trinity Church United Methodist-Beaver Dam. Numerous tributes have poured in from the community, remembering him as the heart and soul of Halloween in Beaver Dam.

Jeff’s passing is a significant loss to his community. His love for Halloween and knack for creating an unforgettable spectacle will be greatly missed.
