Ashley Walters top movies, TV shows and awards

Publish date: 2024-08-30

Ashley Anthony Walters, popularly known by his stage name Asher D, is a British actor and rapper. He was born on June 30, 1982.

He originally gained notoriety as a member of the UK garage band So Solid Crew, whose second single “21 Seconds” reached the top place.
Walters has had a successful acting career as well.

In this piece, we would like to learn about his career particularly his top movies and shows as well as awards and nominations he has amassed.

Ashley Walters’s  top movies and TV shows

Walters first came to prominence in the early 2000s for his parts in Storm Damage (2000), Bullet Boy (2004), Get Rich or Die Trying (2005), and Life and Lyrics (2006).

He then went on to become a household name in the 2010s for his lead role as Dushane Hill in the British crime series Top Boy and as Ronnie Pike Jr. in the Sky One police procedural series Bulletproof, which he co-created with Noel Clarke and Nick Love.

Additionally, he has made guest appearances on British television programs such as Grange Hill, The Bill, Holby City, Doctor Who, Silent Witness, and the 2015 BBC police drama Cuffs.

Ashley Walters awards and nominations

Ashley Walters has won the following awards:

  • 2004 British Independent Film Award for Most Promising Newcomer for his performance in the film Bullet Boy

He has also been nominated for the following awards:

  • 2020 National Film Award for Best Actor in a TV Series for his performance in the TV series Top Boy
