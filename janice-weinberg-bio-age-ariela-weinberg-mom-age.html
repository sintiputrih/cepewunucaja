Meet Janice Weinberg, 90 Day Star Ariela Weinberg’s Mom

As seen on 90 Day Fiance, Janice made her way to Ethiopia to meet her pregnant daughter and ensure she would have no problems. So, upon arriving, while she might have been a little concerned about Ariela and Biniyam’s living conditions, she never ridiculed it.

She was polite, and even visited the local hospital and gave it her approval for Ariela’s birthing. Moreover, she was also was open to learning all about Ethiopia and its culture.

And for this the viewers respected her. But it all changed in the later episodes when she acted too protective of her daughter. Rather than letting Ariela handle her matter, she tried to provide emotional and financial crutches.

However, Janice could be right in her world. Staying in the States would open up better facilities. But this would be right for Janice’s husband Biniyam, who already lost touch with his child by his former marriage to an American woman.

But then again, Janice never believed in Biniyam. She always felt that their relationship will come to an end someday, and she wanted to be close to her daughter when that happened.

Over one of the episodes, Janice even warned Ariela about Biniyam’s ex-girlfriend. In case you missed it, this happened as the mother-daughter duo was sitting in a club and Biniyam stepped up to the state with a gorgeous female partner (who later Ariela relieved was his ex-girlfriend.)

“Better watch out, Ari, Especially when he’s dancing at night, and you’re home alone,” Janice said.

Janice’s grandson Aviel was born in late 2019. However, the revelation was only made a year later.

Janice Weinberg Job

Janice Weinberg might be a reality star, but when she wasn’t working on TV, she offered her time as a consultant for emergency and trauma departments. As of 2021, she was working as an “interim director of emergency department and urgent care” at St. Bernardine Medical Center for almost 3 years. 

Also, she was in the emergency department of HCA Healthcare for almost 4 years then.

Before that, Janice has acted as a director of emergency departments for Jacobi Medical Center, Hartford Health Care – Backus and Plainfield Hospitals, Brookhaven Memorial Hospital Medical Center, Kings County hospital, and Coney Island Hospital.

Besides offering medical consultancy, Janice also took requests over Cameo for “advice, fun shootouts, or any holiday or occasion.”

Her net worth was over $700 thousand as of 2021.

Some of you may not know, but Janice is also a horse trainer. Her horses have won races in 2005 at Delaware Park (USA), 5f 110y, and Delaware Park (USA), 6f.
