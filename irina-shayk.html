Irina Shayk or Irina Valeryevna Shaykhlislamova is an American who is a popular Russian model and actress by profession. According to Models.com, she is an “Industry Icon.”

She gained worldwide attention when she became the first Russian model to appear on the cover of Sports Illustrated’s 2011 Swimsuit Issue.

Irina Shayk Wiki/Biography

Born on 06 January 1986, Irina Shayk’s age is 36 years as of 2022. She was born and raised in a well-settled half-Muslim half-Christian family from Yemanzhelinsk (Chelyabinsk Oblast region), Soviet Union, Russia. She is a Russian by nationality and has her belief in the Russian Orthodox, Christian religion.

She completed her early schooling at a Local High School in Yemanzhelinsk, Russia.

After that, she enrolled herself at the university of marketing in Russia from where she was about to complete her graduation but instead chose a beauty school with her elder sister. From the very beginning of her childhood, she was more interested in modeling and acting rather than in studying.
