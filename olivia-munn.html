Early Life

Olivia Munn was born in Oklahoma City, the United States on July 3, 1980. Olivia’s zodiac sign is Cancer and she is currently 40 years old. Her father’s name is Winton Barrett Munn and her mother’s name is Kimberly Munn. Olivia holds American nationality and belongs to a mixed ethnicity of Chinese, English, Irish, and German. Moreover, Olivia was raised in Vietnam and Tokyo, Japan.

Later, Olivia’s mother migrated to Oklahoma with her. Olivia’s parents divorced when she was two years old and was raised by her mother and stepfather. Olivia has an elder brother named James and two older sisters named Annie and Sara. Olivia’s mother is from Vietnam and arrived as a Vietnamese refugee in the United States in 1975 after the Vietnam War. Later, Olivia’s mother settled in Oklahoma where she met Munn’s father Winton.
