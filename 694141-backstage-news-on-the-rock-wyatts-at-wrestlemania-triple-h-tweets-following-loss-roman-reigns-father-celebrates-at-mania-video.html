Backstage News on The Rock – Wyatts at WrestleMania

Dave Meltzer noted on Wrestling Observer Radio today that The Rock actually requested to work with The Wyatt Family at WrestleMania 32 last night. The Rock had a back-and-forth with the Wyatts before defeating Erick Rowan in a short singles match and then cleaning house with John Cena.
