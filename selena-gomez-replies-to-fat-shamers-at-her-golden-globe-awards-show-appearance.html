Selena Gomez brushed off online fat shamers after her Golden Globes red carpet performance on Tuesday.

The singer, producer, and actress came to Instagram with her date, her younger sister Gracie Teefey, to discuss body positivity.

“I’m a little bit big right now because I enjoyed myself during the holidays,” she said, asking Teefey, “I mean… right?”

“Yeah!” Teefey answered.

Supporters were ready to defend Gomez.

She shouldn’t have to explain why her body looks how it does,” one reader said.

“She’s perfect and doesn’t need to give explanations,” wrote another.

Gomez was nominated for her performance in Hulu’s “Only Murders in the Building.”

In 2022, she had a similar reaction to people commenting on her appearance, saying followers, “Honestly I don’t care about my weight because people bitch about it anyway… ‘You’re too small,’ ‘you’re too big,’ ‘that doesn’t fit,’ na-na-neh-nehhh… I am perfect the way I am. Moral of the story? Byyye!”
