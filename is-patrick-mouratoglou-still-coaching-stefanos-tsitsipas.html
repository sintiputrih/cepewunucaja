Is Patrick Mouratoglou still coaching Stefanos Tsitsipas?

Publish date: 2024-01-18

Stefanos Tsitsipas is a Greek professional tennis player. He has been ranked as high as world No. 3 by the Association of Tennis Professionals, which he first achieved on 9 August 2021, making him the highest-ranked Greek player in history alongside Maria Sakkari.

Stefanos Tsitsipas was born on August 12, 1998 (age 24 years), in  Athens, Greece.

Is Patrick Mouratoglou still coaching Stefanos Tsitsipas?

Patrick Jean André Mouratoglou is a French tennis coach and sports commentator of Greek descent. He was the coach of Serena Williams from June 2012 to 2022. Since Spring 2022, Mouratoglou has been the coach of Simona Halep.

Patrick Mouratoglou is currently the coach of Stefanos Tsitsipas, he has not been a full-time coach since Wimbledon 2021.

Patrick Mouratoglou is one of the most successful coaches currently on the ATP and WTA Tours. He’s coaching top players like Serena Williams.

Patrick Mouratoglou decided to walk away as Serena Williams’ coach recently due to her decision not to participate in the French Open at Roland Garros.
