Biography

Natasha Klauss was born in Barranquilla, Colombia on June 25, 1975. Known for her roles on such Colombian telenovelas as Pasion de Gavilanes (as Sarita Elizondo) and La Venganza (as Sandra Guzman), she received numerous soap opera honors, including a Premio TVyNovelas Award for her antagonistic role as Isabela Montilla on the La Tormenta series. She and Marlene Favela were both in the cast of a telenovela titled Zorro: La Espada y la Rosa. Before pursuing an acting career, she trained as a ballet dancer; however, she was forced to give up her dream of dancing after her knee was severely injured in an automobile crash.
