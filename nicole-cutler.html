Biography

Nicole Cutler was born in Durban, South Africa on June 8, 1972. South African professional dancer who is recognized for her time as a dance coach on the televised dance competition Strictly Come Dancing. She is also known for having held the title of World Amateur Latin-American champion. Both she and Karina Smirnoff performed in Burn the Floor. She used to dance competitively with Andrew Magin prior to moving to the UK in 1990.
