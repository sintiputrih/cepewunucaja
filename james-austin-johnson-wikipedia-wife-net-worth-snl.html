James Austin Johnson Net Worth

James Austin Johnson had above an estimated $600K net worth by Oct 2021.

A Nashville native now living in Los Angeles, James is a comedian who has appeared on Future Man on Hulu, Robbie on Comedy Central, The Last Tycoon on Amazon, Adam Ruins Everything on truTV, and in the movie Hail Caesar.

The last time we heard, James was performing weekly in Los Angeles at Highland Park’s longest-running comedy show, Rod Stewart Live.

IMDB also credited him for Hail, Caesar! (2016) and Blue Like Jazz (2012).

James has had a lifetime of theatrical training. He was only 10 when he started acting. He studied the Sanford Meisner technique in college. Following this, he did many regular appearances as a stand-up comedian and podcast host, but none of these did more to put James on the map than his “Trump complains about Scooby-Doo” video.

James started with direct mimicry, being openly racist and homophobic just as Trump is. But soon, he realized that people were listening and not liking what they were hearing from him. “When I was repeating what Trump said or heightening it—making it more vicious to people, darker.”

That is when James decided to pivot his famous not-written-out and not-rehearsed Trump impressions with a more abstract take.

In the meantime, fans eagerly wait for James to be on SNL alongside two other cast members: Aristotle Athari and Sarah Sherman.
