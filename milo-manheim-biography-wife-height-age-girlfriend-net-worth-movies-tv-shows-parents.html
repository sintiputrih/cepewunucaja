Biography

Milo Manheim (born March 6, 2001), whose real name is Milo Jacob Manheim, is a talented actor and TV personality. He is known for his outstanding acting abilities and charismatic on-screen presence. His exceptional performances have captivated audiences worldwide, particularly his lead role in the popular Disney Channel Original Movie trilogy, Zombies.

Milo Manheim has been passionate about acting since his early years, and his hard work and dedication have paid off handsomely. He gained widespread recognition for his portrayal of Zed in the first installment of the Zombies franchise, which showcased his acting chops and helped him gain a massive following.

However, his participation in the 27th season of the hit show Dancing with the Stars cemented his status as a star. Despite fierce competition, he managed to grab the runner-up spot, thanks to his impressive dancing skills and charming personality.

Born and raised in California, Milo Manheim has worked tirelessly to pursue his dreams and establish himself in the entertainment industry.

Career

Milo Manheim has been a showbiz sensation since early childhood, having begun his acting journey at the tender age of six in an after-school program in Culver City. He starred in a whopping 20 musicals with Liza Monjauze Productions from 2008 to 2017, earning his stripes as a performer.

Milo Manheim‘s first television appearance was in 2009 on the CBS hit series Ghost Whisperer, marking his debut in front of the camera. But he truly made his mark in the entertainment industry by landing the leading role in the Disney Channel Original Movie, Zombies, which premiered on February 16th, 2018. As Zed, the human zombie high school student, he captivated audiences and paved the way for his future success.

The young actor continued to shine, winning the Outstanding Performance in a Leading Role award at the 2017 New York Musical Theatre Festival for his impressive performance in the musical Generation Me. And he didn’t stop there. Milo Manheim took the world by storm as a contestant on the 27th season of the popular dance competition show Dancing with the Stars in 2018, partnered with the talented Witney Carson. Though the duo didn’t win the grand prize, they left a lasting impression and earned a well-deserved second place.

Milo Manheim‘s star power continued to rise as he reprised his role as Zed in the Zombies 2 sequel, which premiered on February 14th, 2020. The Disney Channel’s dedication to the franchise and he is an undeniable talent led to the production of Zombies 3, set for release on Disney+ on July 15th, 2022.

As if that wasn’t enough, in August 2022, Milo Manheim was announced as the lead in a new Paramount+ series called School Spirits alongside co-star Peyton List. It’s clear that his talent and hard work have led him to great heights in the entertainment industry, and there’s no doubt that he has a bright future ahead of him.
