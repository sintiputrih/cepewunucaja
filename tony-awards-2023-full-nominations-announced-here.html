Jessica Chastain, Samuel L. Jackson, Josh Groban lead Tony Awards 2023 nominations

Publish date: 2024-03-24

The 76th Tony Awards are following on the success of "The Lehman Trilogy" and "Company" last year

    The 76th Tony Awards nominations are in! Broadway's annual premiere awards ceremony is back with a new set of leading nods for achievements in plays and musicals over the 2022-2023 season.

    The current star of "Funny Girl," Lea Michele, and last year's winner for Best Performance by a Leading Actor in a Musical in "MJ," Myles Frost, announced the major nominations on CBS Mornings.

    Jessica Chastain, Samuel L. Jackson, Sean Hayes, Ben Platt, Josh Groban, and Audra McDonald are among the major stars in the global entertainment world who picked up nominations for Tony Awards.

    Among the plays, "Ain't No Mo'," "Leopoldstadt," and "A Doll's House" picked up the most nods with six each, while among the musicals, "Some Like It Hot" came out on top with a whopping 13 nominations, followed by "Shucked," "& Juliet," and "New York, New York" with nine each.
