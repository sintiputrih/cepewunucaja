Kate Major Gives Birth, Welcomes Baby Boy Logan With Michael Lohan

Publish date: 0001-01-01

Welcome to the world, Logan Michael Lohan! Michael Lohan and his wife Kate Major have welcomed their second baby boy together, Us Weekly can exclusively confirm.

The new addition arrived at 11:16 a.m. on New Year's Eve, weighing in at 6 lbs., 14 oz. November 16 (11/16), coincidentally, is also Major's birthday.

"Kate and I are ecstatic. A bit late for Christmas but the best gift imaginable," Lohan, 54, tells Us in a statement. "What a way to bring in the new year! God has blessed me with the best children a father could ever ask for — but I'm done!"
