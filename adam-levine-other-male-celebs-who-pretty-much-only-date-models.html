Adam Levine &amp; Other Male Celebs Who Pretty Much Only Date Models

Publish date: 2024-07-14

Handsome male celebrities have the pick of the litter when it comes to the dating pool. Dating is something that can either be really fun or really complicated. For these male celebrities, it seems like something that is far from complicated and a lot of fun. These male celebrities have chosen to spend most of their time in relationships with models!

The models that they date walk down catwalks in fashion shows, pose for magazine covers, as well as catalogs, and star in some of the most high-end advertisements ever. These are some male celebrities who have dated multiple models in their lifetime, and probably will continue to do so.

10 Jude Law

Between 2003 and 2006, Jude Law was in a serious relationship with Sienna Miller, the gorgeous model. After that relationship came to an end, he dated another model named Susan Hoecke in 2007. She is known for being a German lingerie model! In 2008, he started dating an American model named Kimberly Stewart and then fast forward to 2013, he got into a relationship with another model named Alicia Rountree from Mauritius. In 2014, he was dating a Hungarian playboy bunny named Linda Zimeny.

9 Joe Jonas

These days, Joe Jonas is happily married to Sophie Turner who isn’t necessarily a model. She is an extremely talented actress who started acting out a very young age for HBO’s Game of Thrones and grew up right before our eyes. Prior to entering his committed relationship and marriage to Sophie Turner, Joe Jonas dated around-- including Karlie Kloss! The same Karlie Kloss who used to be best friends with Taylor Swift. He also dated a Swiss model named Blanda Eggenschwiler and who could forget his brief relationship with Gigi Hadid?

8 Jason Statham

The relationship between Jason Statham and Rosie Huntington-Whiteley is so wonderful to see. They seem to really make each other happy with their difference in height being the least of their concerns. On every single red carpet, she is noticeably taller than him but that is often times the life male celebrities have to live if they want to date runway models! Prior to getting with Rosie Huntington-Whiteley, Jason Statham also dated a British model named Kelly Brook and an Australian model named Sophie Monk.

7 Leonardo DiCaprio

Well before Gisele Bundchen ended up in her marriage to Tom Brady, she was in a long-term super lovey-dovey relationship with Leonardo DiCaprio. They were the Hollywood IT-couple at the time and paparazzi would go into a feeding frenzy trying to capture the best images of them.

Obviously, things didn’t work out between Leonardo DiCaprio and Gisele Bundchen and he ended up dating a model from Israel named Bar Rafaeli and a German model named Toni Garrn... just to name a few. He’s currently in a relationship with 23-year-old model Camila Morrone.

6 Adam Levine

If anyone deserves to be on this list, it is certainly Adam Levine, who has dated so many models it is pretty much uncountable! Some of those models include Jane Herman Bishop, Maria Sharapova, Rebecca Ginos, Janine Habeck, Arielle Vandenberg, Angela Bellotte, Amanda Setton, Nina Agdal, Behati Prinsloo. In 2011, he serenaded his girlfriend at the time, Anne Vyalitsyna, as she walked down the yearly Victoria’s Secret runway.

5 Billy Joel

Famous musician Billy Joel, has had fair share of models by his side. His relationship with Christie Brinkley is one that fans will talk about the most but that isn’t the only model that he dated. Christie's daughter, Sailor Brinkley-Cook, has followed in those modeling footsteps. Billy Joel also had a date with Elle Macpherson, who posed for Sports Illustrated swimsuit edition in the 80s! Models who get a chance to pose for Sports Illustrated and Victoria’s Secret are definitely sought after.

4 Johnny Depp

Johnny Depp’s most recent relationship ended in a very messy and tragic way with actress Amber Heard. Yes, she is known for being an actress but she is not the only woman Johnny Depp has dated in the past! He also dated petite-sized model Kate Moss, a woman who broke boundaries in the modeling industry for walking down runways and not standing at the average model height of 5’9” and above. He also dated Vanessa Paradis who is known for being a French model as well as a singer and actress.

3 John Legend

Relationship goals are defined when it comes to John Legend and Chrissy Teigen. She is one of the most well-known models in the world and she and John legend decided to tie the knot in 2013. They share two children together and seem to be living a very good life. She is another beautiful model who has posed for Sports Illustrated in the past!

2 Orlando Bloom

Orlando Bloom is happy in love with Katy Perry these days and we couldn’t be happier for him! They have been engaged for a while but had to postpone their wedding ceremony due to the COVID-19 pandemic. They also just welcomed their first born daughter together, Daisy, into the world at the end of 2020 and they seem to be the type of people who would make amazing parents! The fact of the matter is that although Katy Perry is known for being a singer, he has also dated models in the past. He was married to Victoria's Secret Miranda Kerr and even shares a son with her. He also dated Kate Bosworth and Helena Christensen.

1 Mick Jagger

One of Mick Jagger's most well-known relationships was with a model named Jerry Hall. They split after 23 years and four kids together! Another model he dated in the past is named Luciana Gimenez, a gorgeous beauty from Brazil. Mick Jagger's daughter, Georgia May, ended up going down the modeling route once she got old enough too!
