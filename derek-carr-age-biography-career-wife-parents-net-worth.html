Derek Carr Biography

On March 28, 1991, Derek Dallas Carr, a quarterback for the Las Vegas Raiders of the National Football League, was born.

He played collegiate football at Fresno State, where he twice received first-team All-MWC honors.

The Raiders selected him in the second round of the 2014 NFL Draft. The Raiders made the playoffs in 2016 for the first time since the team’s establishment in 2002 thanks to three-time Pro Bowl pick Derek Carr.

He led Las Vegas to yet another postseason appearance in 2021.

Derek Carr siblings and family

The youngest of Rodger and Sheryl Carr’s three children, Derek Carr was born in Bakersfield, California.

When his elder brother, David Carr, was selected first overall by the then-expansion Houston Texans in the 2002 NFL Draft, he and his family left Bakersfield and moved to Sugar Land, Texas.

Derek Carr burst onto the scene with full force when he began playing professionally after a standout collegiate career.
