Fred McGriff: Top 5 Most Expensive Baseball Cards

Publish date: 2024-01-22

The Crime Dog himself, Fred McGriff has certainly left a lasting impression on the hobby of baseball card collecting. In this article, we will take an in-depth look at the five most expensive Fred McGriff baseball cards ever. 


The Legend of Fred Mcgriff in the Baseball Card World


One of the better power hitters was Fred McGriff, and his cards have meant a lot to baseball card collectors everywhere. A career that included at least 30 home runs in a season on ten different occasions led to high demand for his baseball cards. McGriff was a force on the field from Toronto to Atlanta and collectors eagerly snatched up his cards.

 

Top 5 

1. 1986 Donruss Fred McGriff RC #28

The 1986 Donruss Fred McGriff Rated Rookie card is a coveted gem among collectors. As McGriff's only true rookie card, it has maintained its popularity throughout his career. While raw copies of this iconic card are relatively inexpensive, high-grade versions can command prices reaching into the hundreds.

2. 1987 Topps Traded Tiffany #74T Fred McGriff RC

An exemplar of rarity and quality, the 1987 Topps Traded Tiffany Fred McGriff Rookie Card in PSA 10 Gem Mint condition has been a significant highlight in the baseball card market. With its scarcity and pristine state, this card has fetched impressive prices at auctions.

3. 1996 Leaf Signature Autographs Fred McGriff SP

The 1996 Leaf Signature Autographs Fred McGriff SP card stands out as a rare find, with only 1,000 cards in circulation compared to the more standard 5,000. Its scarcity and significance as one of McGriff's best-certified autographs have made it a prized possession for avid collectors.

4. 1986 ProCards FRED McGRIFF Syracuse Chiefs Minor League PSA MINT 9

This minor league gem has also garnered attention due to its scarcity and the historical significance of being linked to McGriff's early career. Its PSA MINT 9 rating adds to its desirability, making it a sought-after addition to any collection 

5. 2024 Leaf Lumber Hall of Fame Card #16 1989

The 2024 Leaf Lumber Hall of Fame Card #16 1989 featuring Fred McGriff is a testament to his enduring legacy. With only 20 cards in existence, this limited edition has captivated collectors, resulting in remarkable prices at auctions
