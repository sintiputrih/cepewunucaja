Aaron Tveit Goes Instagram Official with Girlfriend Ericka Hunter, Writes Sweet Birthday Post

Publish date: 2024-01-27

Aaron Tveit has gone Instagram official with longtime girlfriend Ericka Hunter!

The 37-year-old actor, best known for his work on Broadway and in the USA series Graceland, took to his social media page on Saturday (May 8) to wish Ericka a happy birthday.

For those who don’t know, Ericka is also a musical theater actress and she has been starring alongside Aaron in Moulin Rouge on Broadway. It was just announced that Broadway shows will return in September and we hope to see them back on stage!

Click inside to check out the post…

“My favorite picture of you. My favorite smile. Happy Birthday @erickahunter. I love you!” Aaron captioned the post, which you can see below.

Aaron and Ericka have been dating for years, but they have kept their relationship off of social media until now.
